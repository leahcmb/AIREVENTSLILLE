ABOUT US

We are 4 IESEG students in Master 1 in digital specialization. This is our website for Air'events, an event agency near Lille. We propose events for professionals, companies, local authorities, etc... We also propose ephemeral events for individuals who simply want to take part in an event as a participant and not as an organizer. Motivated and curious, the Air'events team hopes that you will take as much pleasure in browsing this site as we did in creating it.
